About Eternal Branch The Series tells the story how The Main Character could be in both Protagonist and Antagonist's side. In Season 1, we see how the MC becomes the villain and how he comes back. In Season 2, we see who's the culprit behind all the troubles. In Season 3, we learn why the most evil antagonist is so cruel through his backstory. In the Finale, the Protagonists kill the Villain and take his powers. Eternal Branch is a Minecraft Animated Series based on an Animated Music Video made by Richaf Animations. The Series currently has more than 159,522 total views on YouTube. In Arc 1, that cruel and cold Villain who is known to be dead is actually only faking his death by sending his clones to the Protagonist, while the real one roams through the Core of Realm, "The Eternal Branch". He opens the gate through multiverses and causes massacre and destruction everywhere, claiming himself as Archemoros (False God). But luckily, The Memorial Demigod and The Guardian of Eternal Branch successfully defeat him and reset the entire Realm.
